Privacy Policy — Brick Breaker: Candy Pop

Last updated: 2026-06-07 · Publisher: Thuỳ Anh Solution · Contact: thuyanh08091990@gmail.com

This policy explains how the Brick Breaker: Candy Pop app ("the app") collects and uses data. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Data we process

The app requires no sign-in and does not collect your real name, email, phone number, contacts, or location. It processes:

  • Advertising (Google AdMob): device identifiers (including the IDFA on iOS if you allow it), basic device information, and ad interactions, used to serve and measure ads.
  • Leaderboard (Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore): a nickname you choose, your score, and a randomly generated device ID used to keep one record per device. The nickname need not be your real name.

2. Tracking & App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS the app shows the ATT permission prompt before serving personalized ads. If you decline, the app still works and shows only non-personalized ads. Users in applicable regions (e.g. the EEA) also see Google's consent (UMP) form.

3. Third parties

The app uses Google LLC services: Google AdMob (ads) and Google Firebase (leaderboard). Google's data processing follows its policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Children

The app is for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13; it is not in the Kids Category. If you believe your child provided data, contact us to have it removed.

5. Data retention & deletion

Your nickname and score are stored to power the leaderboard. To delete your record, email your nickname to thuyanh08091990@gmail.com.

6. Security

We apply reasonable measures to protect data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated; the new version replaces the old one on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact
